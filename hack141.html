Top Attractions

Panambur Beach
Breathtaking sunsets and clean shores located about 10km north of Mangalore.Famous for the port area and picnic spots, it is spectacular to watch the ships anchored out in the sea waiting for berth in the harbour.

Kudroli Temple
The Gokarnatheshwara Temple, otherwise known as Kudroli Shri Gokarnatha Kshethra,is in Kudroli,Mangalore.It was consecrated by Narayana Guru and is dedicated to Gokarnatha, a form of Lord
